Renault Duster FAQs

Q. What is the ground clearance of the Renault Duster?

A. The Renault Duster has an impressive ground clearance of 210 mm, which is one of the best in its segment.

Q. Is there an automatic transmission available?

A. Yes, the Duster comes with a 6-speed MT and a refined CVT automatic option for the turbo-petrol variants.

Q. Does it have a diesel engine?

A. Yes, Renault continues to offer the legendary 1.5L dCi diesel engine known for its torque and fuel economy.

Q. What are the key safety features?

A. It features 6 airbags, Level 2 ADAS, Hill Start Assist, and Electronic Stability Control as standard in higher trims.

Q. What is the boot space?

A. The Duster offers a healthy boot capacity of 475 liters, ideal for long family road trips.

Q. Is there a 4x4 or AWD option?

A. Yes, the Iconic variant comes with an Intelligent AWD (All-Wheel Drive) system for off-road capability.
